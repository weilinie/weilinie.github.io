Weili Nie

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Eletrical and Computer Engineering at Rice University advised by Ankit Patel. Prior to joining Rice, I did my undergrad degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). I also did my Master degree at Southeast University. During my PhD studies, I have done several internships at Nvidia Research and VMware Research.

Research

I am broadly interested in algorithms and theories in deep learning. Recently, my research has been focused on deep generative models and deep learning interpretability.
