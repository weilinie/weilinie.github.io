Weili Nie

I am an AI Research Scientist at Meta Superintelligence Labs, working on multimodal generation. Previously, I was a Research Scientist in the Fundamental Generative AI Research Team at NVIDIA Research. I received my PhD from the ECE Department at Rice University, advised by Ankit B. Patel.

Research
