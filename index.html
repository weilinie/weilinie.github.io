Weili Nie

I am a Research Scientist in the Machine Learning Research Group at NVIDIA Research, working with Anima Anandkumar. I got my PhD from the ECE Department at Rice University in 2020, advised by Ankit B. Patel. Prior to joining Rice, I got my BS degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) and MS degree from Southeast University. During my PhD studies, I have done several internships at NVIDIA Research and VMware Research.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  GitHub  /  Twitter

Research

My current research is focused on deep generative models, in particular for controllable generation and learning disentangled representations, and their applications to visual reasoning, fairness, and explainability.
